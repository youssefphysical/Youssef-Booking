VERY IMPORTANT.

Add a clean premium help system across the client dashboard.

Requirement:
Every important section should have a subtle help/explanation icon.

Use:

- small ⓘ or ? icon
- soft glass tooltip
- mobile-friendly
- non-intrusive

When user taps it:
Show a SIMPLE explanation in human language.

Examples:

“InBody”
→ “Upload your body scan here to track fat loss, muscle gain, and overall progress over time.”

“Check-ins”
→ “Weekly updates help Youssef adjust your training and nutrition for better results.”

“Progress photos”
→ “Photos help compare your physical transformation more accurately than weight alone.”

“Supplements”
→ “Your recommended supplements and timing based on your current goal.”

“Sessions left”
→ “How many personal training sessions remain in your active package.”

“Verified”
→ “Verified clients completed their profile and uploaded their body analysis.”

VERY IMPORTANT:
Explanations must sound:

- premium
- human
- supportive
- simple

NOT robotic.
NOT technical.
