Critical mobile navbar overflow issue after login.

Problem:
In logged-in mobile state, the top navbar controls are too wide:

- logo/name
- notification button
- language selector
- sign out button
- hamburger menu

Because of this, the hamburger menu on the right becomes partially clipped / pushed outside the viewport.
This is not professional and must be fixed.

Root issue:
The logged-in navbar does not have proper mobile responsive compression logic.
It is trying to fit too many full-size controls in one row.
