1. TERMS & CONDITIONS
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Create or fully refine Terms & Conditions with the following sections:

A. Service Overview
Explain:

- personal training booking platform
- sessions/packages
- scheduling system
- optional assessments
- coaching services

B. Booking Rules
Clearly define:

- booking cutoff logic
- sessions require advance booking
- availability subject to schedule capacity

C. Cancellation Rules
Clearly separate:

- normal cancellation
- emergency cancellation
- rescheduling

State:

- cancellations inside cutoff count as used sessions
- emergency cancellation depends on package allowance
- same-day reschedule only when eligible slots exist

D. No-show Policy
State:

- missing a session without attendance counts as a completed session
- late arrivals do not extend session duration

E. Package Validity
State:

- packages have expiration periods
- unused sessions expire automatically after validity ends
- expired sessions are non-refundable and non-transferable

F. Refund Policy
State clearly:

- completed, expired, or partially used packages are non-refundable
- refunds are not guaranteed once sessions or assessments are used
- administrative fees may apply where legally permitted

G. Membership Responsibility
State:

- gym membership fees are separate from coaching services where applicable
- access to training locations may require active membership

H. Trainer Availability
Protect the business from:

- delays
- emergencies
- gym operational issues
- force majeure events
- weather/traffic disruptions

State:
reasonable efforts will always be made to maintain schedules.

I. Results Disclaimer
State:
Results vary based on:

- consistency
- nutrition
- recovery
- genetics
- lifestyle
- medical condition
- adherence to the program

No guaranteed physical outcome is promised.

J. Health Responsibility
State:
clients are responsible for:

- disclosing injuries
- medical conditions
- limitations
- relevant medications

The platform is not a medical provider.

K. Conduct & Safety
State:
abusive, unsafe, or inappropriate behavior may result in:

- suspension
- refusal of service
- account termination

L. Media & Transformations
State:
photos/progress media require explicit consent before public use.

M. Platform Changes
State:
services, schedules, packages, and policies may evolve over time.
