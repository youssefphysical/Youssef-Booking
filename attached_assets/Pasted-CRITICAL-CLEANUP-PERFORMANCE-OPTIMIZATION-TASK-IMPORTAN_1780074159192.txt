====================================================
IMAGE QUALITY PROTECTION (VERY IMPORTANT)
====================================================

Do NOT reduce image quality.
Do NOT compress existing images in a way that lowers visual quality.
Do NOT downscale hero images, branding images, logos, profile photos, marketing assets, transformation photos, nutrition images, or any customer-facing image.

Optimization must NEVER come from reducing quality.
